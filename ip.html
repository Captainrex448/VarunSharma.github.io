Wood Portfolio Book

In my Junior year I joined an after school course called MakerLab. In this class we worked on creating portfolio books from wood for ourselves. We first started with coming up with our design for the front cover. We each had to take inspiration from nature and I chose a close up image of a leaf. After we traced over our sketches with micron markers, we imported a picture onto Illustrator to fix any line issues. After fixing the sketches in Illustrator we got to laser cutting. Below is the nature pattern I chose and my sketch. I chose to actually etch my design onto the wood instead of cutting it because I felt that would be better for my design.

Wood Portfolio Book V2

This was my most recent project for my IDA class. The project required us to either create a portfolio book or a box with our designs cut from wood. I chose to make another portfolio book as i felt that i could come up with a unique design for it. My design took inspiration from a city grid system with each square representing a building or other structure. I had printed two layers of my design the first layer was the initial design and the second layer was an offset of the first layer .I then glued the two layers together and ended up with my final product.
